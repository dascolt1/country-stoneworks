Who We Are...

Who I am as a man and a mason - I am the third generation of my family on my father’s side who is and has been in the construction and masonry field. I started working as a mason at the tender age of eighteen for my father and later for other companies in the New York/Connecticut area. My only tasks were to mix mud for the masons, bring them stones, bricks, and blocks and to ensure there was always mud for them to lay with. The mud HAD to be the perfect texture and consistency, otherwise the longevity of and the guarantee on the project would be diminished. Similarly, I always watched the masons with admiration and respect, longing to prove to them and myself that no matter the task, big or small, I was ready to start setting stones with them. Finally after five years of not touching a stone, I was given the opportunity to set my first stone on the face of a wall. Ever since that day I have worked with a passion for the art, history, and culture of being a stonemason, as well as being an Italian American from a family who came to this country in pursuit of the American Dream. The way most Italian immigrants knew how to earn their living was as a mason from the Old World in Italy. In this trade, one must be dedicated, passionate and hardworking, as it can be grueling at times. This is why it’s important for us to love what we do! It's not an easy trade, but the end result of a great project coming to fruition is what I strive for as a man and a mason!
